Privacy Policy for LC (Let's Connect)

Last updated: July 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how LC ("we," "our," or "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect a username and password to create and secure your account.
  • Location Data: We collect your device's precise location when you log in or use the main screen of the app. This is used solely to verify that two users completing a task together are physically near one another, so both can receive their reward.
  • Device Identifier: The app generates a random, app-specific identifier stored on your device. This is used, together with location, to confirm that two different users (not the same device) completed a task together.
  • Task and Activity Data: We store which tasks you complete, your progress through the app's stages, and reward/verification status associated with your account.

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate core app functionality, including verifying completed tasks between two users.
  • To maintain your account and progress within the app.
  • To provide customer support if you contact us.
  • To improve the app's features and user experience.

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Sharing

Your information is stored securely using our backend infrastructure provider (Supabase). We do not share your location, device identifier, or account information with third parties except as necessary to operate the app or as required by law.

Data Retention

We retain your account and activity data for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the "Delete Account" option in the app.

Your Choices

  • You may decline location permission, though this will prevent you from completing task verification, a core feature of the app.
  • You may delete your account at any time directly within the app.
  • You may contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data.

Children's Privacy

LC is not directed at children under 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your region), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
